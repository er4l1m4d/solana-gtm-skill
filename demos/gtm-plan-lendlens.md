# LendLens — GTM Strategy

## Executive Summary

LendLens is the simplest way to lend and borrow on Solana. We target retail DeFi users who are intimidated by complex lending interfaces. Our advantage is one-click lending with real-time risk visualization. We aim to capture 1% of Solana lending TVL ($10M) within 6 months of launch.

---

## Positioning & Messaging

**Project:** LendLens
**Problem:** Existing lending protocols on Solana have complex interfaces, hidden risks, and require deep DeFi knowledge to use safely.
**Target Users:** Retail DeFi users who want to earn yield or borrow without the complexity.
**Differentiation:** One-click lending, real-time risk visualization, simplified liquidation alerts.
**Positioning:** "The simplest way to lend and borrow on Solana."

**One-Liner:** LendLens makes DeFi lending accessible to everyone with one-click deposits, real-time risk scores, and instant withdrawals.

## Competitive Analysis

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Kamino | Deep liquidity, strong integrations | Complex interface, no risk visualization | Simpler UI, risk scores |
| Solend | Established, wide asset support | High fees, confusing UX | 3x lower fees, cleaner design |
| Marginfi | Good UX, strong community | Limited assets, no visual risk | More assets, better risk UI |
| Drift (Lending) | Integrated with perps | Secondary feature, not focused | Dedicated lending experience |

**Positioning Strategy:** We win on simplicity and risk transparency. Users who find Kamino/Solend too complex are our primary target.

## Slide 2: The Problem

**The Problem:** DeFi lending on Solana is too complex for retail users.

- Existing protocols (Kamino, Solend, Marginfi) require deep DeFi knowledge
- Users can't easily understand their risk exposure
- Liquidation events happen without warning
- New users are intimidated by complex interfaces

**Who has this problem?**
- Retail DeFi users who want to earn yield or borrow
- Crypto newcomers who find existing protocols confusing
- Users who have been liquidated due to poor risk visibility

**Market:** $2B+ TVL in Solana lending protocols. Growing 50%+ YoY.

**Why Now:** Solana's ecosystem is maturing. Retail users are arriving but existing tools are built for degens, not everyday users.

## Slide 3: Our Solution

**LendLens:** One-click lending with real-time risk visualization.

**What we built:**
- One-click deposit/withdraw (no complex approvals)
- Real-time risk score for every position
- Instant liquidation alerts via push notifications
- Simplified dashboard showing exactly what you earn/owe

**How it works:**
1. Connect wallet (Phantom/Solflare)
2. Select asset to lend or borrow
3. See risk score and expected yield
4. One click to deposit/withdraw

**Differentiator:** We're the only lending protocol that shows your risk in real-time with simple visual indicators.

# Grounded Output Rule

All output from the GTM skill must be grounded in reality. No hype, no empty promises, no generic advice.

## Constraints

### 1. Reference Real Solana Ecosystem Data
- Use actual project names (GMTrade, PYUSD, Tala, Kamino, Drift, Jupiter)
- Reference real metrics (TVL, fees, users from DefiLlama, Dune)
- Cite real ecosystem partners (Jupiter, Raydium, Metaplex, Helius)
- Use real Solana advantages (400ms finality, low fees, 65K TPS)

### 2. No Generic Marketing Advice
- ❌ "Build a strong brand" → too vague
- ✅ "Create a POV worksheet with your contrarian take on [industry]"

- ❌ "Post on social media" → too vague
- ✅ "Post 3-5x/week on X/Twitter using thread format with Solana-specific hooks"

- ❌ "Engage with the community" → too vague
- ✅ "Reply to every comment in the first hour, share other Solana projects' wins, attend Colosseum events"

### 3. No Price Predictions
- ❌ "Your token will 10x"
- ✅ "Focus on TVL growth and user retention for sustainable value"

- ❌ "This will moon"
- ✅ "Target $1M TVL in Month 3 based on comparable protocols"

### 4. No Financial Advice
- ❌ "Invest in this project"
- ✅ "Here are the metrics to evaluate the project's health"

### 5. Realistic Timelines
- ❌ "You'll be worth $100M in 6 months"
- ✅ "Target 1,000 active users and $1M TVL in 6 months based on similar projects"

### 6. Honest Assessment
- Acknowledge competitors' strengths
- Admit market challenges
- Address team gaps
- Discuss risks honestly

## Example: Grounded vs Ungrounded

**Ungrounded:**
> "Your DeFi protocol will revolutionize lending on Solana. With your innovative approach, you'll capture 50% of the market within 3 months. Token price will 100x. Just launch and the community will come."

**Grounded:**
> "Your lending protocol targets retail DeFi users who find current interfaces complex. With one-click lending and real-time risk visualization, you can differentiate from Kamino and Solend. Based on comparable protocols, target $100K TVL in Month 1, $1M in Month 3. Focus on X/Twitter content (3-5x/week) and Discord community building. Track D7 retention as your North Star Metric."

## Why This Matters

1. **Credibility**: Grounded advice builds trust
2. **Actionability**: Specific advice is usable
3. **Honesty**: Realistic expectations prevent disappointment
4. **Solana-Specific**: Ecosystem knowledge shows expertise
5. **Production-Grade**: Matches what judges expect

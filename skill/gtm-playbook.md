# GTM Playbook — Full Go-to-Market Strategy

This framework guides Solana founders through building a complete go-to-market strategy. Based on analysis of successful Solana launches including GMTrade, PYUSD, and Tala.

---

## When to Use

Use this when a user says:
- "Build me a GTM strategy"
- "How should I launch my project?"
- "Help me plan my go-to-market"
- "I need a launch plan for my protocol"

## Execution Mode

First, confirm the user's mode:

**Hackathon Mode (2-3 weeks)**
- Focus: Standout narrative, community building, hackathon demo
- Skip: Long-term sustainability planning, institutional partnerships
- Results: Demo-ready GTM plans that win competitions

**Founder Mode (3-6 months)**
- Focus: Sustainable revenue, user acquisition, investor readiness
- Include: Detailed budget, partnerships, 6-month roadmap
- Results: Real launches with actual metrics

---

## The Framework: 7-Step GTM Process

Walk the user through these 7 steps. For each, ask the guiding questions and build the deliverable incrementally.

---

### Step 1: Define Your Unique Value Proposition

**Guiding Questions:**
1. What problem are you solving? (Be specific — "lending is inefficient" is too vague)
2. Who has this problem? (Retail traders? Institutional investors? Other protocols?)
3. What makes you different from existing solutions? (e.g., GMTrade: "5-9x cheaper fees + RWA support")
4. What's your one-sentence positioning? (e.g., "The On-Chain Robinhood")

**Example Output (GMTrade):**
> **Project:** GMTrade
> **Problem:** Existing perp DEXs have high fees (5-45bps), limited asset selection, and complex UX for retail users.
> **Target Users:** Retail traders who want CEX-like experience on-chain.
> **Differentiation:** 0.5bps fees (5-9x cheaper), ~90 markets including RWA (gold, oil, FX), pooled liquidity model.
> **Positioning:** "The On-Chain Robinhood — Trade Everything on Solana."

**Deliverable for Step 1:** 1-page positioning document

---

### Step 2: Understand Your Competition

Use `competitor-analysis.md` for depth, but summarize here:

**Guiding Questions:**
1. Who are your top 3 direct competitors?
2. What do they do well?
3. Where do they fall short?
4. How do you win where they lose?

**Example Output (GMTrade-style):**
> | Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
> | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
> | Drift Protocol | Deep liquidity, active traders, order book model | Higher fees, slow new market listing, single asset focus | 5-9x cheaper fees, faster RWA listing |
> | Jupiter Perp | Massive user base, aggregated liquidity | Single pool = higher risk, no RWA | Multi-pool with risk isolation |
> | Kamino | Strong lending + leverage | No perps, no RWA | Cross-product synergies |
> | GMX (Ethereum) | Proven model, deep liquidity | High gas, ETH-only, no Solana | Solana speed + lower costs |

**Deliverable for Step 2:** Competitive positioning matrix

---

### Step 3: Define Your Channels

**Guiding Questions:**
1. Where does your target audience hang out? (X/Twitter, Discord, Telegram, YouTube, newsletters?)
2. What's your primary channel? (Where you'll focus most effort)
3. What's your secondary channel? (Where you'll maintain presence)
4. What's your tertiary channel? (Long-form content)

**Example Output:**
> **Primary:** X/Twitter (tech founders, crypto natives, Solana community)
> **Secondary:** Discord (community building, support, beta testing)
> **Tertiary:** YouTube / Newsletter (deep dives, technical updates)
> **Bonus:** Colosseum community (if hackathon participant)

**Channel Priority Matrix:**
| Channel | Purpose | Frequency | Content Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| X/Twitter | Awareness, engagement | 3-5x/week | Threads, updates, memes |
| Discord | Community, support | Daily | AMAs, updates, support |
| YouTube | Education, trust | 1-2x/month | Tutorials, deep dives |
| Newsletter | Retention, updates | 2x/month | Product updates, insights |
| Telegram | Announcements | As needed | Launches, partnerships |

**Deliverable for Step 3:** Channel strategy document

---

### Step 4: Build Your Content Calendar (30 Days)

Use `social-content-planner.md` for depth. Key phases:

**Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days 1-14)** — Education & Teasing
- Day 1-3: Announce the project + explain the problem
- Day 4-7: Technical deep-dives (how it works, architecture)
- Day 8-10: Introduce the team (personal stories, why this problem)
- Day 11-14: Countdown + launch date announcement

**Phase 2: Launch Week (Days 15-21)** — Hype & Momentum
- Day 15: LAUNCH DAY — live announcement
- Day 16: Partner announcements (integrations, collaborations)
- Day 18: User testimonials / early metrics
- Day 20: AMA session

**Phase 3: Post-Launch (Days 22-30)** — Trust & Scale
- Day 22-24: Metrics update (TVL, users, volume)
- Day 25-27: Roadmap preview (what's next)
- Day 28-30: Community spotlight / user stories

**Solana-Specific Hooks:**
- Reference Solana ecosystem partnerships (Jupiter, Raydium, Orca)
- Tag relevant Solana accounts (@solana, @SuperteamDAO, @ColosseumHQ)
- Use Solana-specific hashtags (#Solana, #SolanaEcosystem, #SolanaSummer)
- Reference Solana metrics (TPS, low fees, finality)

**Deliverable for Step 4:** 30-day content calendar with specific post ideas

---

### Step 5: Plan Your Budget & Resources

**Guiding Questions:**
1. What's your total marketing budget? (Hackathon: $0-500, Founder: $5K-50K)
2. Where will you allocate spend? (Ads, influencers, events, content?)
3. What internal resources do you have? (Team members, time, skills)

**Example Output (Founder Mode):**
> | Line Item | Budget | Purpose |
> | :--- | :--- | :--- |
> | Content Creation | $1,000 | Professional graphics, video editing |
> | Influencer Marketing | $2,000 | 3-5 X/Twitter KOLs in Solana ecosystem |
> | Community Management | $500 | Discord/Telegram moderation tools |
> | Ads (X/Farcaster) | $1,500 | Targeted user acquisition |
> | Events (Solana conferences) | $2,000 | Booth, swag, networking |
> | **Total** | **$7,000** | |

**Example Output (Hackathon Mode):**
> | Line Item | Budget | Purpose |
> | :--- | :--- | :--- |
> | Content Creation | $0 | DIY with AI tools |
> | Community | $0 | Organic Discord/Twitter |
> | Demo Video | $0 | Screen recording + voiceover |
> | **Total** | **$0** | |

**Deliverable for Step 5:** Budget allocation document

---

### Step 6: Define Your Success Metrics

Use `metrics-dashboard.md` for depth. Key metrics:

**Pre-Launch:**
- Waitlist signups
- Social following growth (X/Twitter followers)
- Newsletter subscribers
- Discord members

**Launch:**
- Total Value Locked (TVL) — for DeFi
- Active users / wallets
- Transaction volume
- Fees paid
- Trading volume (for perps)

**Post-Launch:**
- User retention rate (D1, D7, D30)
- Open Interest (for perps) — GMTrade prioritizes OI over volume
- LP yield sustainability
- Community growth rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Solana-Specific Benchmarks:**
| Metric | Good | Great | Exceptional |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| TVL (Day 30) | $100K | $1M | $10M+ |
| Active Users | 100 | 1,000 | 10,000+ |
| X/Twitter Followers | 500 | 5,000 | 50,000+ |
| Discord Members | 100 | 1,000 | 10,000+ |

**Deliverable for Step 6:** KPI dashboard with targets

---

### Step 7: Create Your 6-Month Roadmap

**Guiding Questions:**
1. What are your next 3 product milestones?
2. What partnerships do you need to secure?
3. What does success look like at Month 6?

**Example Output (GMTrade-style):**
> | Month | Milestone | Key Activities |
> | :--- | :--- | :--- |
> | Month 1 | Launch + initial liquidity | Deploy on mainnet, seed liquidity, initial marketing push |
> | Month 2 | RWA expansion | List gold, crude oil, major FX pairs |
> | Month 3 | Mobile app launch | Ship mobile app for position management |
> | Month 4 | Institutional partnerships | Secure 3+ institutional LPs |
> | Month 5 | New market expansion | Add stocks, indices, more commodities |
> | Month 6 | Full feature parity | Match CEX capabilities, cross-margin, portfolio margin |

**Deliverable for Step 7:** 6-month roadmap with key milestones

## Key Principles

1. **Be Specific**: Generic advice is useless. Reference real Solana projects and ecosystem data.
2. **Be Actionable**: Every step should produce a deliverable the founder can use immediately.
3. **Be Honest**: No hype, no empty promises. Reference real benchmarks and realistic timelines.
4. **Be Solana-Specific**: Leverage Solana's unique advantages (speed, low fees, ecosystem partnerships).
5. **Be Iterative**: A GTM plan is a living document. Revisit and refine as the project evolves.
